Property Description

Open and spacious with multiple sliders leading out to the travertine patio and stunning views. Large Master with king size bed and in suite bathroom with bidet. Second and third bedrooms have queen size beds with quality mattresses. The kitchen is new with granite, stainless steel appliances and custom cabinetry. There is a lovely heated pool and a dock with a lift. A short walk to historic Matlacha with shops, restaurants and art galleries.
